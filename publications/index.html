  3. NoTeS-Bank: Benchmarking Neural Transcription and Search for Scientific Notes Understanding
    Aniket Pal, Sanket Biswas, Alloy Das, Ayush Lodh, Priyanka Banerjee, Soumitri Chattopadhyay, Dimosthenis Karatzas, Josep Lladós, and C. V. Jawahar
    arXiv preprint arXiv:2504.09249, 2025
    Abs arXiv Bib

    NoTeS-Bank is a benchmark for evaluating neural transcription and search on handwritten scientific notes containing equations and diagrams. It defines two tasks — evidence-based VQA with localization and open-domain VQA with domain classification — to assess vision-language fusion, retrieval, and multimodal reasoning.
