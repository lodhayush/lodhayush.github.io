**NoTeS-Bank** is a benchmark for evaluating how well vision-language systems
understand **handwritten scientific notes** — pages dense with equations,
diagrams, and domain notation that defeat standard OCR pipelines.

The benchmark defines two tasks:

- **Evidence-based VQA** — answer questions with bounding-box localization of
  the supporting region.
- **Open-domain VQA** — answer questions with domain classification, testing
  retrieval and multimodal reasoning.

Built with collaborators from ISI Kolkata, CVC Barcelona, and IIIT Hyderabad;
the companion **Handwritten Notes Understanding Challenge** ran at ICDAR 2025.
